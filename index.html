About Me

I’m a very motivated Front-End Developer with strong tech skills and detailed oriented personality.

I've enjoyed the experience of teaching several undergraduate and graduate classes. Moreover, this allowed me to improve my communication skills.

I would describe myself to be very effective and having an efficient mind, a passion of helping others, so I can be a great asset in any web development project.
